## Mad Mac: Revised TSO Corridors
Disables many of the blocks in the TSO Corridor sets and cleans up their
G-menu block groups for a cleaner interface and reduced analysis paralysis.

The TSO Corridor and Round Corridor expansion mods are nothing if not thorough,
and the variety of options are a big improvement over the vanilla blocks for
building compact ships and stations.
At the same time, the sheer number of blocks and groups can be a little overwhelming,
especially for newer players, which frequently prevents me adding them to my servers and save games.

This mod offers a compromise between completeness and user-friendliness,
removing most of the lesser-used blocks and consolidating the block
variant groups in the G-menu to just a handful.

### Mod Load Order
Because it overwrites some of the TSO Corridor configuration, this mod must be loaded after the TSO Corridor mods i.e.:
* Placed **above** all TSO Corridor mods in the mod list for **local** save games
* Placed **below** all TSO Corridor mods in the mod list on a **dedicated server**
